About Us—Thunder Run Expeditions

Since our first launch into the rapids, Thunder Run Expeditions has been driven by passion and courage. What began as a daring leap into untamed waters has grown into a community of explorers who thrive on challenge, discovery, and unforgettable river journeys. With expert guides and a commitment to safety, every trip is more than an adventure—it’s a story waiting to be lived.

History

Thunder Run Expeditions was founded in 2001 by two adventurers who fell in love with the roaring rapids below the Falls. What started with one raft and a wild dream has grown into a trusted name in whitewater rafting. Our certified guides, top-tier safety, and unforgettable river journeys make every trip a story worth telling.
